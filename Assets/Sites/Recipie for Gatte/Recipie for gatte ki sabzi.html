Gatte ki Sabzi recipe by Arvind

Ingredients:

For the gatte:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ajwain (carom seeds)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup curd
  • 1 tablespoon ghee or oil
  • Water, as needed

For the gravy:

  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 cup curd
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves

Instructions:

1. Make the Gatte

  1. Mix in curd and ghee or oil until a dough forms. Add water if necessary.
  2. Shape the dough into small balls, about the size of marbles.

2. Cook the gatte:

  1. Boil water in a pot. Add gatte and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until cooked through.
  2. Drain gatte and set aside.

3. Make the gravy:

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a large pot over medium heat. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cinnamon stick, cloves, and green cardamoms. Sauté for 30 seconds.
  2. Add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and coriander powder. Sauté for another 30 seconds.
  3. Add curd and bring to a simmer.
  4. Add cooked gatte and simmer for 5-7 minutes, or until gravy thickens.
  5. Stir in coriander leaves and salt to taste.

Serve:

Enjoy hot with roti or naan.
